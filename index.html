About me

Hi! My name is Ekaterina. I have been working in web development for over 3 years. I completed a course in front-end development and now help projects become more user-friendly and visually appealing.

My approach is simplicity and functionality. I love clean code, well-designed interfaces, and attention to detail. In my work, I use modern approaches: semantic layout, CSS Grid and Flexbox, mobile-first responsive layout.

I spend my free time reading books or learning about new technologies. I dream of working on useful products that make people's lives better.
